Privacy Policy

Introduction

Hey Jude's Lawn Care ("we," "us," "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide to us, particularly through Facebook ads and other interactions with our services.

Information We Collect

1. Basic Information: We collect essential contact information such as your first and last name, email address, and phone number.

2. Address Details: To provide quotes and our services, we collect your address.

3. Service Information: We gather details related to the lawn care services you require to better tailor our offerings to your needs.

4. Cookies and Tracking: We use cookies and similar technologies from Facebook ads to track your interactions with our site and ads for marketing and analytics purposes.

Cookies

A "cookie" is a small file stored on your device that contains information about your device. We may use cookies to provide basic relevant ads, website functionality, authentication (session management), usage analytics (web analytics), and to remember your settings, and generally improve our websites and Services. We use session and persistent cookies. Session cookies are deleted when you close your browser. Persistent cookies may remain even after you close your browser, but always have an expiration date. Most of the cookies placed on your device through our Services are first-party cookies, since they are placed directly by us. Other parties, such as Google, may also set their own (third-party) cookies through our Services. Please refer to the policies of these third parties to learn more about the way in which they collect and process information about you.

If you would prefer not to accept cookies, you can change the setup of your browser to reject all or some cookies. For more information, please visit https://www.aboutcookies.org/

You may also set your browser to send a Do Not Track (DNT) signal. For more information, please visit https://allaboutdnt.com/

How We Use Your Information

1. Billing: Your information is used to process and fulfill your lawn care service orders, including recurring billing and scheduling.

2. Communication: We may use your contact information to communicate with you regarding your inquiries, service updates, or to address any questions or concerns you may have.

3. Marketing: We may use your information to send you promotional materials, offers, or information about new products and services.

4. Security Measures: We prioritize the security of your personal information and implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

Your information may be shared with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you. These providers are contractually obligated to maintain the confidentiality and security of your personal information.

7. Changes to This Policy: This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons.

8. Contact Us: If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at jude@judeslawncare.com.
