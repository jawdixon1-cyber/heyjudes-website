Lawn Maintenance Frequency

Weekly Service: Recommended for clients who want their lawn looking pristine and freshly cut at all times. Weekly mowing helps maintain a consistently neat appearance and reduces the chance of clumping or visible grass clippings.

Bi-Weekly Service (Every Other Week): Bi-weekly mowing is a good option for clients who want basic lawn upkeep and are comfortable with sometimes having a a less manicured look. Please note that with bi-weekly service, there is a higher chance of clumping or visible clippings/debris. This is due to natural factors such as varying rainfall, temperature, sun and the growth rate of your specific grass type. Sometimes grass grows faster, sometimes slower, and when the grass grows longer grass can cause more noticeable clippings when cut.

By choosing bi-weekly service, clients acknowledge and accept this trade-off: the lawn will be maintained, but it may not always appear perfectly manicured like with weekly service.
