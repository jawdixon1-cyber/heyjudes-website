Services Included

The following services are included in this agreement. All services are scheduled and performed by Hey Jude's Lawn Care according to the frequency listed below.

Lawn Maintenance
Every other week • Mar - Oct
  • Mow entire lawn at the proper height for your grass type
  • String trim around all stationary obstacles (fences, trees, beds, mailbox, etc.)
  • Edge along all sidewalks, driveways, and curbs for clean lines
  • Edge around landscape beds to create a clean divider between grass and beds, keeping grass and weeds from creeping in
  • Blow all clippings off driveways, sidewalks, porches, and hard surfaces into the lawn
Leaf Maintenance
Every other week • Nov - Feb
  • Blow all leaves off landscape beds, porches, walkways, driveways, and hard surfaces
  • Mulch leaves into the lawn to return nutrients to the soil
  • Trim grass as needed to keep the lawn tidy during leaf season
  • Keep beds and the entire property looking clean
Stick Pickup
Every visit • Year-round
  • Pick up all sticks on the property every visit
  • Disposed off-site
Hedge Trimming
3x per year • Apr, Jul, Oct
  • Shape and trim all shrubs, bushes, and hedges on the property
  • Remove all clippings and debris from beds and surrounding areas
  • Maintain natural shape while keeping growth in check
Aeration & Overseeding
1x per year • October
  • Core aerate the entire lawn with commercial-grade equipment
  • Overseed at 5 lbs per 1,000 sq ft using LESCO Tall Fescue Select Blend — a certified, professional-grade seed mix bred for strong root development, drought tolerance, and year-round color in our climate
  • Provide clear aftercare instructions for watering and first mow timing
  • Plugs are left on the lawn to break down naturally and feed the soil
Pine Straw Installation
1x per year • February
  • Install fresh long-leaf pine straw in all landscape beds
  • Roll and tuck edges for a clean, finished border
  • Clear straw off bushes, plants, and any hard surfaces
  • Even coverage at proper depth for weed suppression and moisture retention
  • All old straw is worked into the base layer for a uniform look
