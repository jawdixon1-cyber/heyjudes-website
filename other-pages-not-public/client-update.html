Weekly vs Biweekly

Weekly delivers the sharpest curb appeal. Less growth removed each visit means a clean, even finish every time.

Biweekly works too, but depending on your yard, grass type, rain, and other factors, there's naturally more growth between visits. That extra growth, especially during wet weeks, can mean heavier clippings that show up as light rows in the turf. Biweekly visits are also priced higher per visit due to the extra time and equipment wear.

Want the sharpest look? Text WEEKLY anytime and we'll switch you over.
