What's New This Season

Better Billing
Recurring maintenance like mowing is now billed automatically at the end of each month for visits completed that month. Not the 1st of the month, not the 1st of the week — at the end of the month, after the work is done. Tied to our schedule, so no errors. One-time services are quoted and billed separately. We accept card and ACH — no cash or check. Card on file required.
Better Communication
You'll get an automatic text the day before your scheduled mow, an on-the-way text when we're headed your way, and a reschedule notice if anything changes. Sometimes we get ahead of schedule and show up sooner than planned — but you'll always get that on-the-way text so you're never surprised.
These automated texts come from a different number than Jude's personal line. Each message includes a reminder not to reply to that number — we don't receive messages there. For anything you need, always text Jude directly.
Client Portal
Your own online portal to request work, approve quotes, view upcoming appointments, view current invoices, and change your payment method — all in one place. When you add your card on file, take a look around and see what's there.
