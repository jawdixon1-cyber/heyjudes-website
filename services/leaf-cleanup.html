Why Leaf Cleanup Matters

A thick layer of leaves doesn't just look messy. Left too long, it smothers your grass, traps moisture, and creates the perfect conditions for fungus, mold, and pest problems.

Regular leaf removal keeps your lawn healthy, your beds clean, and your property looking maintained. It also prevents leaves from clogging gutters, downspouts, and drainage areas around your home. Once cleared, many homeowners follow up with aeration & overseeding to restore any thin spots.

Clean lawn. Clean beds. Clean surfaces. That's the standard.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in a leaf cleanup?
Our leaf cleanups include full removal from your lawn, landscape beds, hard surfaces, and along the foundation. We blow, rake, collect, and haul everything away. Your property is left clean and leaf-free.
Do you haul away all the leaves?
Yes. We collect and haul away all leaves and debris. Nothing gets left behind in piles or pushed to the curb.
How long does a leaf cleanup take?
Most residential leaf cleanups are completed in a single visit, typically within a few hours. Properties with heavy tree coverage or large lots may take longer, which we'll communicate upfront.
When is the best time to schedule a leaf cleanup?
In Rock Hill, fall cleanups are most common from late October through December when leaves are dropping heavily. We also do spring cleanups to clear anything left over from winter. You can schedule anytime your property needs it.
Can I combine a leaf cleanup with other services?
Yes. Many clients pair a leaf cleanup with mulch installation, bed refreshes, or the start of a recurring mowing plan. We can bundle services into a single quote.
Do you offer recurring leaf cleanup during fall?
Yes. For properties with heavy tree coverage, we can schedule multiple visits throughout the fall season so leaves don't pile up and smother your lawn. We'll recommend a schedule based on your property.
