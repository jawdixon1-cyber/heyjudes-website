Retaining Wall FAQ

How much does a retaining wall cost in Rock Hill?
Pricing varies based on wall length, height, material choice, and site conditions like slope and drainage. Most residential projects fall between $2,500 and $15,000+. The best way to get an accurate price is to request a free on-site consultation.
What materials do you use?
We work with a variety of materials including concrete block, natural stone, and timber. We'll recommend the best option based on your property, budget, and the look you're going for.
Do retaining walls need drainage?
Yes. Proper drainage is critical to the longevity of any retaining wall. We install drainage behind every wall we build to prevent hydrostatic pressure and failure.
How long does installation take?
Most residential retaining walls take 2–5 days depending on size and complexity. We'll give you a clear timeline during your consultation.
Do I need a permit for a retaining wall?
In Rock Hill, walls over 4 feet typically require a permit. We'll handle the permitting process for you if needed.
Do you offer warranties?
Yes. Our work is backed by a craftsmanship warranty. We stand behind every wall we build.
